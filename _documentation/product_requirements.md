# Product Requirements: Personal Bio Page

## 1. Overview
A clean, minimalist personal website that introduces me to the world.

## 2. Features
- **Profile Section:** A placeholder circular photo and my name.
- **About Me:** A short paragraph describing who I am.
- **Social Links:** Buttons for Twitter (X) and GitHub.
- **"Hire Me" Button:** A button that opens an email to me.

## 3. Design Vibe
- Dark mode by default.
- Large, bold typography.
- Mobile friendly (looks good on phones).
- centered layout.
